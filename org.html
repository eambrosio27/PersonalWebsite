Mariachi La Joya Del Sur

In 2022, I was brought into this organization by a friend to help them found the first ever collegiate mariachi group at UC San Diego. After our first year where we were trying to get our foot off the ground, I was chose to become the co-president for the 2023-2024 school year! My previous musical experience and experience in starting my own music groups helped me lead this organization into becoming one of the most active LatinX Organizations on campus with over 100 members and 40 active members!

•Recruited 100+ members, gained 1000+ followers, & helped raise over $2000 in funding in less than 2 years

• Developed and implemented organizational structures into the eboard using GSuite to ensure good communication, task management, and lay the foundations for success for future generations of board members

• Planned and co-developed organization website and email newsletter using UCSD servers
